# MONTA — Design System

> **Weirdly cute. Seriously fun.**
> Handmade toys with big personality.

MONTA is a playful direct-to-consumer brand selling **handmade, claymation-style collectible toys** — lumpy, googly-eyed creatures with loads of character. The brand voice is cheeky and warm; the visual world is built from warm cream "graph paper," chunky rounded orange display type, a leafy brand green, and bright primary-ish card colors (sky blue, bubblegum pink, sunny yellow) decorated with hand-drawn doodle sparkles and sun-ray ticks.

This design system reconstructs MONTA's foundations and a marketing/e-commerce UI kit so design agents can produce on-brand pages, shop screens, slides, and assets.

## Content Fundamentals

**Vibe.** Loud, friendly, a little weird. The brand leans into "ugly-cute" — it celebrates imperfection and personality over polish. Copy should feel like a fun friend, never corporate.

**Casing.** Hero/display copy is **ALL CAPS** and exclamatory: *"WEIRDLY CUTE / SERIOUSLY FUN."* Supporting copy is sentence case and lowercase-friendly: *"Handmade toys with big personality."* Navigation uses Title Case (Home, Shop, Contact Us).

**Person.** Talks *to* the customer ("you", "your desk"), and refers to toys as if they're alive — they're "adopted," they have "personality," they're little creatures, not products. Social proof is phrased warmly: *"Adopted by 100+ Happy Humans."*

**Punctuation.** Periods used emphatically inside short headline fragments ("SERIOUSLY FUN."). Exclamation allowed but not overused.

**Tone examples (on-brand):**
- "Weirdly cute. Seriously fun."
- "Handmade toys with big personality."
- "Adopted by 100+ Happy Humans"
- "Meet the crew." / "Adopt me" / "Take one home"
- "No two are exactly alike — that's the point."

**Avoid:** stiff e-commerce phrasing ("Add to cart for the best value"), superlative marketing-speak, jargon, anything that sounds mass-produced. Keep sentences short and punchy.

**Emoji:** none in the source. The doodle sparkle/star and sun-ray ticks play the role emoji would — keep decoration to those drawn marks rather than unicode emoji.

## Visual Foundations

**Color.** Warm cream paper (`#fffbef`) is the canvas for everything. Two brand anchors: a vivid **orange** (`#ff8a1e`) for headlines and primary CTAs, and a leafy **green** (`#4d8e02`) for the logo, nav, and secondary buttons. A trio of bright **card colors** — sky blue, bubblegum pink, sunny yellow — frame the product photography and add the playful energy. Text is a deep olive-near-black (`#2c3a1b`) with olive-green secondary tones, never pure gray.

**Type.** **Titles only** use the **pixel faces**: Latin in *Pixelify Sans 700*, CJK in *Cubic 11* (俐方體11號, bundled at `fonts/Cubic_11.woff2`) — the `--font-display` stack falls through per-glyph so "Python 入門課程" mixes both automatically. **Body / UI text** uses *Fredoka* for Latin and **微軟正黑體 (Microsoft JhengHei)** for Chinese (`--font-ui`, falling back to PingFang TC / Noto Sans TC on non-Windows) — body Chinese is **not** pixel. The `monta` logo wordmark stays in the legacy rounded face (*Baloo 2*, exposed as `--font-display-round`).

**Mascot — 拍拍 (Pai-Pai).** A pixel **snake** (Python ↔ snake) built from rounded-square segments: lime body (`#90d24a`), yellow eyed head (`#ecd84e`). It curls, arcs, and weaves around lockups. A photographic clay version is in `assets/paipai.png`; the recolorable pixel version is generated by `logos/snake.js` and showcased in `logos/logo-explorations.html`.

**Backgrounds.** The signature surface is **warm cream with a faint, slightly-perspective grid** (like graph paper receding toward the horizon). Sections are flat cream — **no photographic backgrounds, no gradients on backgrounds.** Color appears in discrete card shapes, not washes.

**Imagery.** Real, tactile **claymation/clay toy photography**, brightly lit, warm, saturated, set on solid bright color cards. Photos are playful and slightly imperfect (fingerprints, lumps visible). Product cards are **rounded squares, gently rotated** (±4–8°) and overlapped to feel hand-arranged.

**Doodle accents.** Hand-drawn **4-point sparkle stars** (orange + tan) and small **sun-ray tick clusters** scatter around hero imagery. These are the brand's decorative signature — sparse, asymmetric, hand-placed. Used to add energy around focal points, never in dense patterns.

**Corner radii.** Generous everywhere. Pills for nav and buttons (`999px`); rounded squares (~20–28px) for product cards; medium rounding for inputs. Nothing is sharp-cornered.

**Borders.** Thin (1–2px) `#e7dcc0` hairlines, often as a **rounded outline capsule** (the nav bar is an outlined pill floating on cream). Outlines are warm, low-contrast — structure without heaviness.

**Cards.** Two flavors: (1) **product cards** = solid bright color, big radius, slight rotation, soft drop shadow; (2) **content cards** = cream/white with hairline border and a soft warm shadow. No colored-left-border cards. No glassmorphism.

**Shadows.** Soft and warm (`rgba(74,90,20,…)`), never harsh black. Buttons use a **chunky "hard" bottom shadow** (a solid offset in the darker brand color, e.g. `0 4px 0 #3c7300`) that compresses on press — a toy-like, tactile button.

**Animation.** Bouncy and lively. Entrances use a slight overshoot (`cubic-bezier(.34,1.56,.64,1)`); hovers lift and scale up a touch; the doodle sparkles can twinkle. Motion is friendly, never slow or sober. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

**Hover states.** Lift + slight scale-up (`translateY(-2px) scale(1.02)`); fills deepen one step (orange→orange-deep, green→green-deep). Links underline or shift to brand green.

**Press states.** Button **compresses down** onto its chunky shadow (`translateY(3px)`, shadow shrinks to `0 1px 0`) and the fill goes one shade darker — a physical "squish."

**Transparency / blur.** Minimal. The brand is flat and opaque. No frosted glass. Slight transparency only for subtle overlays (e.g. doodle ticks at lowered opacity).

**Layout.** Centered, generous whitespace on cream. A floating outlined nav pill at top. Hero is a big stacked display headline with supporting copy off to one side and a playful, overlapping cluster of product cards below/beside. Asymmetry and a hand-arranged feel are intentional.

## Iconography

The source page is **glyph-light** — it relies on type, color, and photography rather than a dense icon set. Observed icon usage:

- A **green circular "+" button** (used in the social-proof avatar stack — "add / join").
- **Doodle marks** drawn as brand decoration: 4-point sparkle stars and short sun-ray tick clusters. These are illustrative, not a functional icon set.
- The **logo** is a wordmark ("monta") in the rounded display style, brand green.

There is **no built-in icon font, SVG sprite, or emoji** evident in the source. For functional UI icons (cart, search, menu, chevrons, social), this system **substitutes [Lucide](https://lucide.dev)** (`https://unpkg.com/lucide@latest`) — a rounded, friendly, consistent-stroke open-source set that matches MONTA's soft personality. **SUBSTITUTION FLAG:** Lucide is a stand-in, not confirmed brand iconography; use a **2–2.5px stroke, rounded caps/joins** to stay on-brand. Decorative sparkles/ticks should be kept as the brand's own drawn marks (provided as small inline SVGs in the UI kit), not replaced by Lucide.

Cropped real assets in `assets/`:
- `monta-logo.png` — wordmark (cropped from hero; prefer recreating in Baloo 2 green where possible)
- `toy-blue.png`, `toy-pink.png`, `toy-yellow.png` — the three hero product photos
- `happy-humans.png` — avatar social-proof stack

UI kits:
- **`ui_kits/website/`** — MONTA marketing + e-commerce website kit (`index.html` interactive demo, JSX components, README).
